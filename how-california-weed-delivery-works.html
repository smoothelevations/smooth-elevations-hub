How California Weed Delivery Works

California pioneered legal cannabis delivery. This guide explains the basics so you can order with confidence.

Regulations & ID

Only licensed retailers may deliver cannabis. You must be at least 21 years old (or 18 with a medical recommendation) to place an order. Drivers verify ID upon delivery and cannot leave products unattended.

Ordering

Ordering is similar to online shopping. Browse menus on services like Weedmaps, select items, and check out. Payment is usually accepted in cash or digital methods permitted by the retailer.

Delivery Zones

Delivery companies define their own zones. Smooth Elevations covers Sacramento and suburbs like Elk Grove, Carmichael, Fair Oaks and more. See our Service Areas page for the full list.

Tips for a smooth experience

  • Have your ID ready when the driver arrives.
  • Order to a private residence; public drop‑offs are prohibited.
  • Be available at the delivery address during the estimated window.
