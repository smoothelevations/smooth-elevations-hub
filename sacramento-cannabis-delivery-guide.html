Sacramento Cannabis Delivery Guide

Wondering how to order cannabis delivery in Sacramento? This guide walks you through the process and what to expect. As the capital city of California, Sacramento has embraced adult‑use cannabis while maintaining strict regulations to keep consumers safe.

Is cannabis delivery legal in Sacramento?

Yes. Licensed services like Smooth Elevations operate under California’s Bureau of Cannabis Control. Deliveries are permitted to adults 21+ and patients 18+ with a valid medical recommendation. Delivery drivers must verify your ID at drop‑off.

  3. Provide your delivery address in Sacramento or one of our nearby service areas.
  4. Complete checkout and be ready to show a government‑issued ID upon arrival.
