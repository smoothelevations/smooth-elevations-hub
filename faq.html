Frequently Asked Questions

Who can order cannabis delivery?

Adults 21+ with a valid government ID, or medical patients 18+ with a physician recommendation, may place an order.

What areas do you serve?

We deliver throughout Sacramento and cities like Elk Grove, Florin, Lemon Hill, Vineyard, Rancho Cordova, Carmichael, Fair Oaks, Citrus Heights, Antelope and Rio Linda. See our full service area list.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders arrive within 60–90 minutes. Transit times may vary based on distance and traffic.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Payment options include cash and select digital methods. You’ll see available methods during checkout.

Do I need a medical card?

No. Recreational customers 21+ can order without a medical card. Patients aged 18–20 must provide a doctor’s recommendation.

How is my privacy protected?

We deliver discreetly in unbranded vehicles. Your personal data is never shared or used for marketing beyond your order.

Can you deliver to public places?

No. California law requires deliveries to private residences or businesses where the recipient is legally allowed to possess cannabis.

Where can I see your menu?

Our full menu is on Weedmaps. You can also browse and order directly on our website.
